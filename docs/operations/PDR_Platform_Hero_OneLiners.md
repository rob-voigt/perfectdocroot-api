> PerfectDocRoot is a governed execution platform that enables deterministic AI-assisted workflows.
> The platform replaces unstructured prompt interactions with a structured model built on **domains, contracts, artifacts, and runs**, producing outputs with verifiable provenance.
> By combining governance, artifact-based inputs, and deterministic execution, PerfectDocRoot allows AI systems to operate in environments requiring reproducibility, auditability, and operational traceability.
